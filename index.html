Product Management

Product Vision

What customer problem does the product solve? Which market or market segment does the product address? How is the product going to benefit the company? What product is it? what makes it stand out? What is your purpose for creating the product?

Market Research and GTM Strategy

Collect information about the target market and it's segments. Analyse market size, competitors, and customer behaviors. Use customer feedback and surveys, research using existing products, and benchmark data from competitors, analysts, and Industry sources. Differentiate the product from competitors

SWOT and PESTLE

Identify internal strength and weaknesses along with external opportunities and threats. Analyse the macro-environment using the PESTLE that impact the product development. SWOT and PESTLE provides holistic view of the Product's potential to success.
